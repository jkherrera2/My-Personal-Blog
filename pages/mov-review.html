MOVIE REVIEWS

OF SOME MOVIES I'VE WATCHED

The Adam Project

The Adam Project is such a good movie with excellent fight scenes. The father and son relationship struck me the most because I can relate to it. Moreover, Ryan Reynolds and Walker Scoobell performed great as each other.

Shutter Island

This movie is such a mindblowing piece. It takes you to a rollercoaster ride creating confusion in every scene. It could make you insane right after they present the plot twist of this movie. I just loved how it kept me thinking throughout the movie.

The Notebook

I liked how they made this movie as bittersweet as possible. It just shows how love is not always sunshines and rainbows. Things might not go as we planned together with our partners but the plot twist shows that things get better when love perseveres.

Zombieland 2

This movie is not as good as the first film. Although, I they made sure that the stories of the character are well-built as the movie progresses. The zombie scenes are boring. The plot is good enough.

The Butterfly Effect

A movie about love to family, to a special someone, and to self. I enjoyed this movie because it is very interesting as they incorporated the time travel. I found the alternate endings so creative.

The Prestige

Christopher Nolan never fails me when it comes to movies like Memento and The Prestige where the movies are filled with best plot twists. Hugh Jackman and Christian Bale really did well and gave justification to this movie.

Doctor Strange 2

This movie is one of my most anticipated movie in 2022. I can say that it did not meet my expectation because the movie just lacks better storyline and it is very predictable. Nevertheless, I commend the lead actors for great performance.

Saving Private Ryan

The action, storyline, and great acting are enought to say that this is one of the best war movie out there. It includes great soundtrack, spectacular cinematography, splendid screenplay, and brilliant performance by Tom Hanks.

Good Will Hunting

This movie taught me to look at life in a new perspective. This depicted how a broken man could go through serious character development like no other in life. I will never forget the scene where a man brought down his fences and free himself from the burdens he had.
